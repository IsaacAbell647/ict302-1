For different tpyes of scams there are different places to report them.
## Where to Report
* #### Federal Trade Commission (FTC): Click [HERE](https://reportfraud.ftc.gov/) to file a report to help the FTC build cases against scammers.
* #### Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3): Click [HERE](https://www.ic3.gov/) to report scams to the FBI. This helps laws enforcement track trends and investigate crimes.
* #### Identity Theft: Click [HERE](https://www.identitytheft.gov/) if you are a victim of identity theft, report it to IdentityTheft.gov for a personalized recovery plan.
* #### Local Law Enforcement: Contact your local police or the FBI's local field office to report crimes. 
## For specific types of scams
* #### IRS scams: Report fake IRS, Treasury, or tax-related emails to the IRS by forwarding them as an attachment or providing the email header.
* #### Spam text messages: Report spam texts by forwarding them to 7726 (which spells "SPAM").
* #### Financial or investment scams: Contact the [Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/complaint/) to report financial scams related to services like credit cards, loans, or debt collection.
